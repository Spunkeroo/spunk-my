How to Make Money with Websites in 2026

Websites are still one of the most reliable sources of passive income available. In 2026, with AI-assisted content creation and new monetization channels, the earning potential of even small niche sites has grown significantly. Here are the income streams that are working right now.

1. Affiliate Marketing — The Backbone

Potential: $100 – $10,000+/month per site

Affiliate marketing is recommending products and earning a commission when someone buys. It's the highest-converting monetization method for content sites because it's baked into the content naturally.

The top affiliate programs in 2026:

  • Amazon Associates — 1–10% commission, massive product catalog, instant trust. Use tag=spunk01-20 in links. Every site should have Amazon links.
  • Fiverr Affiliates — $15–150 per referral. Promote freelance services. Great for design, development, and creative niches.
  • Coinbase — $10 per signup when the referred user buys crypto. Perfect for any finance or crypto content.
  • NordVPN — 30–40% commission, recurring. One of the highest-paying affiliate programs for tech content.
  • Leonardo AI — Commission on AI image generation signups. Growing rapidly in 2026.

2. Display Advertising

Potential: $5–50 RPM (per 1,000 pageviews)

Display ads are passive — once you add the code, they run forever. Start with Google AdSense, which accepts new sites. As you grow, upgrade to Ezoic (10k sessions/mo required) or Mediavine (50k sessions/mo) for 3–5x higher RPMs.

A site getting 100,000 pageviews/month on Mediavine at $20 RPM earns $2,000/month from ads alone. Stack this with affiliate income and you have a real business.

3. Digital Products

Potential: $500 – $50,000+/month (no inventory, 90%+ margins)

Sell ebooks, templates, courses, printables, or tools. Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, and Payhip all take small cuts and handle payment processing. Create once, sell forever.

  • Ebooks: $9–49 each. A 5,000-word guide on a niche topic sells well if the SEO is right.
  • Templates: Notion templates, Figma files, spreadsheets — $5–99 each.
  • Mini-courses: $97–497. Even a 5-video course on a specific skill sells well.

4. Sponsored Content

Once your site has traffic and a Google ranking, brands will pay to be featured. Sponsored posts typically earn $50–500 per article depending on your domain authority and niche. Finance, crypto, health, and tech niches pay the most.

5. Crypto Monetization

The 2026 Edge

Accept crypto tips and payments directly. Add a Bitcoin or USDC wallet address to your site footer — even small sites get occasional tips. Run a faucet or game that drives engagement. SpunkArt's spunk.bet uses this model: free-to-play crypto games that build a community and brand loyalty.

6. Lead Generation

Some niches — insurance, legal, home improvement, finance — pay enormous amounts per lead. A single qualified insurance lead is worth $30–200. If you rank for "best homeowner's insurance in [city]," you can sell leads to local agents at premium rates.

The Income Stack Strategy

The most profitable sites in 2026 use ALL of these methods together. Here's a sample income stack for a site getting 50,000 monthly pageviews:

  • Ezoic display ads: ~$600/month
  • Amazon affiliate: ~$400/month
  • NordVPN/Coinbase affiliate: ~$200/month
  • Digital product sales: ~$300/month
  • Sponsored post (1–2/month): ~$200/month
  • Total: ~$1,700/month from one site
Multiply that by 10 sites and you have a $17,000/month business. This is the web empire model.
